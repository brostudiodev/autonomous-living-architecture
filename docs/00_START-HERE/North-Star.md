# North Star: Automation-First Living

## The 2026 Vision

The goal is to move from "Automation" to true **Autonomy** across all aspects of life, allowing me to focus 100% on high-value work, creativity, and strategic growth.

## Core Domains for Autonomy
I am engineering systems to handle the following domains without manual intervention:
- **Wealth:** Finances, expense tracking, and savings optimization.
- **Environment:** Smart home management and 24/7 monitoring.
- **Logistics:** Shopping, grocery procurement, and household supplies.
- **Nutrition:** Food planning, cooking management, and inventory.
- **Career:** Professional management (hours tracking, job searching, and career intelligence).
- **Physicality:** Health monitoring, biometric tracking, and workout orchestration.
- **Transport:** Car maintenance, inspections, and expense management.
- **Infrastructure:** A self-healing homelab that diagnoses and repairs itself.
