# SOP: Weekly Training Review

## Purpose
Standardize the weekly review of HIT training consistency and biometric progression.

## Procedure
1. Open Grafana Health Dashboard.
2. Verify all sessions from the last 7 days are logged.
3. Check average TUT vs. previous week.
4. Update goal status in Obsidian Weekly Note.
