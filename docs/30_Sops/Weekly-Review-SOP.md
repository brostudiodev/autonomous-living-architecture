# SOP: Weekly Review

## Purpose
Weekly strategic reflection and planning to maintain momentum toward quarterly goals.

## Procedure
1. Receive CEO Weekly Briefing on Telegram (Sunday 8 AM)
2. Review Obsidian Weekly Note (generated by `autonomous_weekly_manager.py`)
3. Update "Starved Goals" status
4. Set top 3 priorities for next week
5. Archive stale tasks (Sunday 10 AM via `G11_task_archiver.py`)

## Automated Flow (Sundays)
| Time | Automation | Output |
|------|------------|--------|
| 06:00 | `autonomous_weekly_manager.py` | Obsidian Weekly Note |
| 08:00 | `G11_ceo_weekly_briefing.py` | Telegram Executive Summary |
| 10:00 | `G11_task_archiver.py` | Stale task cleanup |
