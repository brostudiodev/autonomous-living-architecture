# SOP: Calendar Operations

## Purpose
Manage autonomous calendar event creation and availability checking.

## Procedure
1. Send natural language request to Telegram bot (e.g., "Check my availability tomorrow morning").
2. Verify Digital Twin response.
3. For event creation, provide details: "Add lunch with Oliwia on Monday at 1pm".
4. Confirm entry appears in Google Calendar.
