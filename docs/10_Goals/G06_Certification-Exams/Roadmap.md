# Roadmap (2026)

## Q1 (Jan–Mar)
- [ ] Identify target certifications and required exams (e.g., cloud platforms, cybersecurity, AI/ML)
- [ ] Research study materials and resources for chosen certifications
- [ ] Establish a structured study plan with measurable milestones
- [ ] Integrate study progress tracking into a productivity system (G11)
- [ ] Schedule first certification exam (if applicable for Q1)
- [ ] Document learning resources and study strategies for future reference (G09)

## Q2 (Apr–Jun)
- [ ] Complete preparation and take the first wave of certification exams
- [ ] Analyze exam results and identify areas for improvement
- [ ] Adjust study strategies based on performance
- [ ] Begin preparation for second wave of certifications
- [ ] Update G10 (Automated Career Intelligence) with new certifications and skill data

## Q3 (Jul–Sep)
- [ ] Complete preparation and take the second wave of certification exams
- [ ] Explore practical application of certified skills in projects (e.g., contribute to G04)
- [ ] Network with professionals holding desired certifications
- [ ] Document project case studies showcasing newly acquired skills (G09)

## Q4 (Oct–Dec)
- [ ] Evaluate overall progress towards certification goals for the year
- [ ] Document lessons learned and strategy for 2027 certification planning
- [ ] Ensure all certification data is captured in G10 (Automated Career Intelligence)
- [ ] Provide a summary of new capabilities and expertise to G12 (Meta-System)

## Dependencies
- **Systems:** G11 (Productivity Time Architecture for study scheduling/tracking), G10 (Automated Career Intelligence for skill impact analysis)
- **External:** Exam providers, online learning platforms
- **Other goals:** G09 (Complete Process Documentation) for documenting study guides and exam strategies.
