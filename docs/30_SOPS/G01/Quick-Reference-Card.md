# G01: 60-Second Logging Cheat Sheet

## After Workout: 3 Steps

### 1. Open: `G01_Training_Journal` Google Sheet

### 2. Log Sets (tab: `sets`)
For EACH exercise, add ONE row:

date | workout_id | exercise_id | weight_kg | tut_s | max_effort | form_ok | notes


**Example:**

2026-01-23 | hit_upper_a | ex_lat_pulldown | 70 | 92 | TRUE | TRUE | signal: increase


### 3. Log Session (tab: `workouts`)
Add ONE row:

date | workout_id | location | duration_min | days_since | recovered_1_5 | mood_1_5 | notes


**Example:**

2026-01-23 | hit_upper_a | gym | 52 | 3 | 4 | 4 | good session

## Common Workout IDs
- `hit_upper_a` (chest/back/shoulders/arms)
- `hit_lower_a` (legs)
- `hit_fullbody_a` (everything)
